LiTime: 6P Answer

Asked and answered. Kept here as the record behind the 4P decision.

ANSWERED Verdict: 4P, not 6P

LiTime replied that the 24V 100Ah is rated max 4 in parallel. Their words: 6P "exceeds this recommended limit", is "not supported by our warranty", "may compromise the BMS's ability to effectively balance and protect the batteries", and is "not advised for safety and warranty reasons".

Why the cap exists: to ensure BMS coordination and prevent excessive inrush current, uneven current sharing, and imbalance between packs. They called our fusing / pre-balance / diagonal-bus / pre-charge plan "excellent practices" but said "even with these precautions, the fundamental BMS and battery chemistry limitations remain."

Torque, confirmed: M8 terminal bolts = 7 to 10 N·m. Do not overtighten.

Result: main bank = 4P (in warranty), the other 2 batteries run the shed freezer system →
